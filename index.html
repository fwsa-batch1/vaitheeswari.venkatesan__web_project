Welcome to Martial Arts Game !

Controls:

  • LeftArrow : To Kick
  • RightArrow : To Punch
  • DownArrow : To Jump Down
  • UpArrow : To Jump Up
  • W : To Walk

Buttons:

  • Mode : To change between Day and Night mode
  • Sound : To play Music
  • Refresh : Starts Game from First
  • Home : Goes back to HomePage

How To Play? :

  • Pass through all the monsters and reach the end point without getting eaten by the monsters.
  • You have to pass through without getting eaten by the monsters and before the score becomes zero .
  • Jumping deducts two points.
  • Kicking and Punching monsters each earns 10 points.
  • Total points will be calculated as your Score.
